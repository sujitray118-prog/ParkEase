Our Features

ParkEase combines smart technology and real-time data to provide a seamless parking experience for both drivers and parking owners.

📍 Live Location Tracking

Detect your current location and explore nearby parking spaces on an interactive map.

🅿️ Real-Time Parking Availability

View available parking spaces that are updated directly from the Firebase database.

🔒 Secure Authentication

Separate accounts for drivers and owners with secure Firebase login and signup.

🏠 Owner Dashboard

Parking owners can add, view, edit, and manage their parking spaces easily.

⚡ Instant Updates

Any changes made by owners appear immediately for drivers without manual database management.

📱 User Friendly Interface

A clean and simple design that makes finding parking quick and hassle-free.
